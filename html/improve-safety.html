Cammatics can also make any car a “connected car” providing safety benefits to drivers such as alerts to dangerous road conditions or accidents ahead, weather hazards or car maintenance requirements. Drivers can be steered away from potential problems with features like lane- change monitoring, rear view cameras and autonomous braking.

Safer driver makes safer fleets and surroundings. To limit the number and severity of collisions fleet managers are leveraging the power of our solution to produce individual scorecards, create driver- specific training curricula, and recognize and reward their safest drivers.
